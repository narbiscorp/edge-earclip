Difficulty & Strobe

        Difficulty only changes the zone thresholds (Low/Med/High coloring) and the lens opacity curve — the raw coherence score stays comparable across all levels, matching HeartMath convention. Higher levels require more coherence to reach the green zone and open the lens. Lens Darkness Limit (0xA2) caps maximum tint for breathing & coherence programs — feedback still scales 0→limit based on signal. Strobe Frequency (0xAB) sets flash rate for Program 4 and standalone strobe (1-50 Hz). Strobe Duty (0xAC) sets dark fraction per cycle (10-90%). All three persist to flash and override firmware defaults on next boot.

          Rate

          Inhale
          40% / 60%
        

        
      

      

        Controls fixed-rate breathing modes (hall-selected Breathe program and Program 4 when Adaptive Pacer is OFF). Doesn't affect PPG Programs 2/4 when Adaptive is ON — those track your measured respiration. Saved to device flash. Firmware v4.14.30+.
      

    

    
Adaptive Pacer

      
      

        When ON, the breathing pacer in Programs 2 and 4 starts at 6 br/min and then adapts each cycle to match your detected respiration rate (15-second average, clamped 3-10 br/min). When OFF, pacer stays at fixed 6 br/min. Firmware v4.14.32+.
